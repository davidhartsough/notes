## Learning to Love

### The Skills of **Love**, **Connection**, **Synergy**

<details>
<summary>[Full List]</summary>

1. **Mindfulness**, **Serenity**, **Equanimity**
   - Calmness: to be calm
   - Peacefulness: to be peaceful
   - Tranquility: to be tranquil
   - Harmony: to be harmonious
   - Serenity: to be serene
   - Regulation: to regulate
   - Reframing: to reframe
   - Equanimity: to be equanimous
   - Mindfulness: to be mindful
2. **Presence**, **Awareness**, **Attention**
   - Presence: to be present
   - Awareness: to be aware
   - Attention: to be be attentive and give attention
   - Concentration: to concentrate
   - Engagement: to engage
3. **Openness**, **Understanding**, **Acceptance**
   - Listening: to listen
   - Empathy: to be empathetic and empathize
   - Receptiveness: to be receptive and receive
   - Curiosity: to be curious
   - Open-mindedness: to be open-minded
   - Recognition: to recognize
   - Understanding: to be understanding and understand
   - Learning: to learn (and gain insight)
   - Openness: to be open
   - Acceptance: to be accepting and accept
4. **Gratitude**, **Appreciation**, **Admiration**
   - Gratitude: to be grateful and give gratitude
   - Recognition: to recognize and give recognition
   - Appreciation: to appreciate and give appreciation
   - Thankfulness: to thank and give thanks
   - Praise: to praise and give praise
   - Affirmation: to affirm and give affirmation
   - Encouragement: to encourage and give encouragement
   - Admiration: to admire and give admiration
   - Fondness: to be fond
   - Respect: to be respectful and give respect
5. **Care**, **Kindness**, **Compassion**
   - Care: to be caring and give care
   - Kindness: to be kind and give kindness
   - Compassion: to be compassionate and give compassion
   - Benevolence: to be benevolent and give benevolence
   - Fairness: to be fair
6. **Altruism**, **Generosity**, **Support**
   - Altruism: to be altruistic
   - Consideration: to be considerate and give consideration
   - Support: to be supportive and give support
   - Service: to serve
   - Empowerment: to empower
   - Help: to be helpful and give help
   - Aid: to aid and give aid
   - Generosity: to be generous
   - Giving: to be giving and give
   - Sharing: to be sharing and share
7. **Communication**, **Vulnerability**, **Authenticity**
   - Communication: to be communicative and communicate
   - Responsiveness: to be responsive and respond
   - Expression: to be expressive and express
   - Vulnerability: to be vulnerable
   - Openness: to be open
   - Authenticity: to be authentic
   - Honesty: to be honest
8. **Joy**, **Positivity**, **Playfulness**
   - Joyfulness: to be joyful and share joy
   - Positivity: to be positive and share positivity
   - Fun: to have fun
   - Playfulness: to be playful and play
   - Laughter: to laugh and share laughter
9. **Reconciliation**, **Apology**, **Forgiveness**
   - Reconciliation: to reconcile
   - Apology: to apologize
   - Forgiveness: to forgive
10. **Trust**, **Reliability**, **Commitment**
    - Trust: to trust
    - Reliability: to be reliable
    - Commitment: to commit

</details>

### Love for Others

With others, we _consistently_ strive to:

1. Be Mindful, Calm, Equanimous
2. Be Present, Aware, Attentive
3. Be Curious, Understanding, Accepting
4. Give Gratitude, Appreciation, Admiration
5. Give Care, Kindness, Compassion
6. Be Altruistic, Generous, Supportive
7. Be Communicative, Vulnerable, Open

_Often_, we strive to:

8. Be Joyful, Positive, Playful

_Sometimes_, we must:

9. Reconcile, Apologize, Forgive

With _some_ people, we should:

10. Give Trust, Reliability, Commitment

<br />

### Self-Love

With ourselves, we _consistently_ strive to be:

1. Mindful, Calm, Equanimous
2. Present, Aware, Attentive
3. Curious, Understanding, Accepting
4. Grateful, Appreciative, Affirming
5. Caring, Kind, Compassionate

And _often_, we strive to be:

6. Joyful, Positive, Playful

<br />

### Altogether

The skills of both self-love and love for others:

1. Mindfulness, Serenity, Equanimity
2. Presence, Awareness, Attention
3. Openness, Understanding, Acceptance
4. Gratitude, Appreciation, Admiration
5. Care, Kindness, Compassion
6. Joy, Positivity, Playfulness

The skills more specific to loving others:

7. Altruism, Support, Generosity
8. Communication, Vulnerability, Authenticity
9. Reconciliation, Apology, Forgiveness
10. Trust, Reliability, Commitment
