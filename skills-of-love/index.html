Love for Others

With others, we consistently strive to:

  1. Be Mindful, Calm, Equanimous
  2. Be Present, Aware, Attentive
  3. Be Curious, Understanding, Accepting
  4. Give Gratitude, Appreciation, Admiration
  5. Give Care, Kindness, Compassion
  6. Be Altruistic, Generous, Supportive
  7. Be Communicative, Vulnerable, Open

Often, we strive to:

  1. Be Joyful, Positive, Playful

Sometimes, we must:

  1. Reconcile, Apologize, Forgive

With some people, we should:

  1. Give Trust, Reliability, Commitment

Self-Love

With ourselves, we consistently strive to be:

  1. Mindful, Calm, Equanimous
  2. Present, Aware, Attentive
  3. Curious, Understanding, Accepting
  4. Grateful, Appreciative, Affirming
  5. Caring, Kind, Compassionate

And often, we strive to be:

  1. Joyful, Positive, Playful

Altogether

The skills of both self-love and love for others:

  1. Mindfulness, Serenity, Equanimity
  2. Presence, Awareness, Attention
  3. Openness, Understanding, Acceptance
  4. Gratitude, Appreciation, Admiration
  5. Care, Kindness, Compassion
  6. Joy, Positivity, Playfulness

The skills more specific to loving others:

  1. Altruism, Support, Generosity
  2. Communication, Vulnerability, Authenticity
  3. Reconciliation, Apology, Forgiveness
  4. Trust, Reliability, Commitment
